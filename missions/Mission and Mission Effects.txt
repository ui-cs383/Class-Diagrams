Missions are dependent on Player and Character. 
Missions have the Attributes of name, missionType, and restrictSide.
Missions should have the functions getType(Name), checkRestriction(Name), checkSide(restrictSide), assignMission(Character). 

Actions are dependent on Environ, Missions, and Character.
Actions have the attributes resultList, drawSize, continueToDraw, shiftLoyalty, missionSuccess, bonusResult.
Actions have the functions: getEnvironSize(), getMissionType(), drawCard(drawSize), continueDraw(), applyActions(resultList[missionType]), checkActionLetter(missionType), calculateBonus(), calculateShift(missionSuccess, bonusResult), loyaltyShift(shiftLoyalty).
